Add Outcomes in the Outcomes Screen in the Matrix/Scale Definitions Tab

The Outcomes Screen is accessed by clicking the Outcomes Button on the Matrix/Scales Definitions Tab in the Scales section. 

  1. Set the Scale and the Benchmark in the top to fields that you want to assign the Outcome to.
  2. Click the ADD  Button in the right hand corner to activate the fields at the bottom
  3. Enter the specific Outcome you want the User to have access to while completing the assessment.
  4. Enter the point value of the Outcome
  5. Click SAVE.

 

Delete Outcomes in the Outcomes Screen in the Matrix/Scale Definitions Tab

  1. Select the Outcome you would like to delete.
  2. Click the DELETE  Button in the same row on the right hand side.
  3. Confirm that you would like to delete the Outcome by clicking YES.

This is permanent and there is no way to recover the Outcome once you click YES.  You should not delete an Outcome that has been used in an application.
