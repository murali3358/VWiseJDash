If you phone the Live Help Desk and don't get a live person, please know that your call is important to us.  Help Desk Experts may be on the line with another customer or another support call.  Please leave a message and someone will return your call just as quickly as possible. 

Our Help Desk team  has the expertise to listen and immediately determine if the situation is User related or System related.  Many User related situations are resolved at the time of the call.  There are occasions where our programmers or developers need to step in.  Regardless of the required time frame our staff will clearly identify the expected effort required to meet the situation and need.   Please be prepared for a request of print screens and/or reports that will allow us to identify the support issue and resolve it.  

Many of our customers are email ready!  When sending a support ticket by email please include as much as information as possible so that our team can resolve your support issue just as quickly as poosible.  We find that the #1 reason support tickets cannot be resolved in the first round is that we have not received enough detailed information.
