This module handles all of the adminstrative screens and the data entry screens needed to:

  • Design and Maintain your Site Schedule
  • Manage your recruitment data in Client Intake, including Income Verification and Medical Emergency information
  • Design Client Tracking - Master and Component Tracks
  • Manage PIR Control
  • Design and Maintain all PIR Logic Associations
  • Manage Enrollment and Withdrawal Records
  • Work with your Wait List
  • Monitor Average Daily Attendance
  • Design and Maintain Bus Master and Transportation Routes
  • Create Staff to Client Associations
  • Post Attendance and track and monitor CACFP Meal Reimbursements
