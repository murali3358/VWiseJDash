Staff Bulk Posting

Staff Bulk Posting Screens allows the User to track course, training and credential work about all staff, whether it relates to the PIR Report, Section B or not.  Selecting the Category and then Site and Positional Category allows you to post to multiple staff at the same time. 

    

Once you have selected the staff you want to post the record to click the ADD  Button to open the screen below.

Complete the fields and save the record.  This record will post to all the selected staff in the previous screen.
