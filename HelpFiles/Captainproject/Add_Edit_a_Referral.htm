Add a Referral to the Referred From/To Details section of the Service/Activity Posting Screen

Locate the toolbar within the Referral Section of the Serivce Activity Posting Screen. 

It looks like this: 

Click on the ADD  Button in order to access the Agency Referrals selection screen.  Let's look at that below.

  1. Enter the date of the referral you're attaching
  2. Select FROM or TO in the Referred From/To field
  3. Check the row of the agency referral you need.  You do this by left mouse clicking the cell to the left of the CODE column.  Once you do that a GREEN CHECK MARK will appear telling you that is the referral agency you selected.
  4. Click OK

 

Edit a Referral to the Referred From/To Details section of the Service Activity Posting Screen

Locate the toolbar within the Referral Section of the Serivce Activity Posting Screen. 

It looks like this: 

Click on the EDIT    Button in order to access the Agency Referrals selection screen.  You will notice it is the same screen that you use in ADD mode. 

At the Agency Referral selection screen you can change the date, the FROM/TO field or uncheck the agency referral selected and then select another one.

  • You can edit the saved date
  • You can edit the Referred From/To field selection
  • Locate the green check that has selected the referral name if you want to edit that.
  • Clicking on the green check will remove it. 
  • You can then make the new selection you want to change. 
  • Don't forget to click SAVE. 

When you return to the main Service/Activity Posting screen this edited entry will hold a row positon. 

 

Delete a Referral to the Referred From/To Details section of the Service/Activity Posting Screen

Locate the toolbar within the Referral Section of the Serivce Activity Posting Screen. 

It looks like this: 

Click on the DELETE  Button in order to access delete mode. 

This mode is quick and unforgiving.  You cannot recover any data that has been deleted from the system.  There's no nevermind mode.  The system immediately asks you - are you sure you want to delete?  You click OK.  That's it!  Nothing more.  It's gone. 

Let's be certain you understand how the system does this.  Once you click the delete button the system is already locked on one particular entry.  Look at the image below to see that. 

See the blue highlighted row in the Referred From/To Details screen that is behind the Delete msg?  That's the entry that will be deleted.  Make it a habit to always confirm what is being deleted BEFORE you click OK.

Deleting a referral is permanent and that data cannot be recovered.
