This report is run just once after the rollover to the new program year.  Once the benefits have been updated in the system (by CAP Systems) the letters will identify all clients who have been certified or denied in the Control Card.  Changing the Certified Status will determine what letters are generated. 

Re-Run Letters – if by chance the letters are lost, you can complete a re-run.  The system will open a panel to pick a stored report run and reprint what was previously done.
