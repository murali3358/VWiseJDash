Add New Household Member in the Client Intake Screen - required if there is more than one person living in the household

Now you're just beginning to set our own personal style and rythmn in place for how you are entering our data.  You've completed the primary applicant and now you need to move on to any addtional household members that are, equally, an important part of the application. 

At the top of the Client Intake Tab locate the ADD Button.  Let's look at an image of the Tool Bar.

When you hover over the ADD  Button it will confirm that you are going to Add a Household Member. 

The Tabs that are available for adding household members to an existing application are just a little bit different. 

Let's look at the Member Details Tab image.

Notice that now we have Member Details Tab available instead of the Applicant Details.  Why?  The system stays just one step ahead of you.  The system knows that you cannot have two Applicants in one application so it automatically gives us the correct Member Details Tab when adding additional household members.  You will need to complete this section of every household member. 

Don't forget to answer any questions that are available.  This ensures that the system will tag a household or a household member if there are other services or even another program they would benefit from.  Let the system go to work for you! 

You can now SAVE that and move on the the Employment Tab for this household member.  We don't need to review the Tab image, it's exactly the same.  Don't forget to click SAVE when you are done.

As you can see this section zips right along.  You just have to add in the additional member and respond to their personal demographic questions, employment and done!  Repeat this for every member of the household.
