Validate a Service Plan 

Once you have added your Service Plan and designed the CA and MS that you want Users to have access to, you must Validate the Service Plan.  Remember, active Service Plans that are added to the system are displayed in the Service Plan Admin Screen as BLUE font record rows.  This tells the you that the Service Plan has not been validated and is not available for Users to interact with.

Look in the lower right hand corner of the image below.  You will find the validate service plan button you use to validate the plan.  If the system identifies the service plan has no CA or MS a msg will present telling you that you must add CA or MS in order to validate the plan.

A RED Font row in the list box tells the User that the Service Plan is INACTIVE.
