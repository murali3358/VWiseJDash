Add a Service Plan in Service Plans  section of the Service/Activity Posting Screen

Locate the toolbar at the top of the Serivce Activity Posting Screen Tab. 

It looks like this: 

 

Click on the ADD  Button in order to access the Service Plan working screen.  Let's look at that below.

 

  • Service Plan Field  - select the Service Plan you want to assign to the application
  • Site - select the SITE you want to assign
  • Case Worker - select the name you want to assign
  • Complete the Start Date and Estimated Completion Date.  You will enter the Actual Date at a future time.
  • Click SAVE.

After you have added the Service Plan and clicked SAVE the working screen will NOT close.  The bottom section of the working screen will load with the pre-designed critical activities and milestones that make up the Service Plan.  Let's look at that new section of the working screen.

The working screen now has it's own Toolbar where you can ADD, EDIT, and DELETE records for the Critical Activityies and Milestones (activities and goals) your client in working toward and achieving.  SAVE frequently.  Users will update Service Plans all throughout the time you are working with the household. 

Edit a Service Plan in Service Plans  section of the Service/Activity Posting Screen

Editing a Service Plan allows the User to add additional data to an existing plan (update it) or change existing data that is already there. 

Locate the toolbar at the top of the Service/Activity Posting Screen Tab.    

It looks like this: 

 

Click on the EDIT    Button in order to access the Service Plans working screen.  You will notice it is the same screen that you use in ADD mode. 

At the working screen you can change the date, the FROM/TO field or uncheck the agency referral selected and then select another one.

 

Delete a Service Plan in the Service Plans section of the Service/Activity Posting Screen

Locate the toolbar within the Referral Section of the Serivce Activity Posting Screen. 

It looks like this:

 

Click on the DELETE   Button in order to access delete mode. 

This function is quick and unforgiving.  You cannot recover any data that has been deleted from the system.  There's no nevermind mode.  The system immediately asks you - are you sure you want to delete?  You click OK.  That's it!  Nothing more.  It's gone. 

Let's be certain you understand how the system does this.  Once you click the delete button the system is already locked on one particular entry.  Look at the image below to see that. 

See the blue highlighted row in the Service Plan CA/MS DEtails working screen that is behind the Delete message?  That's the entry that will be deleted.  Make it a habit to always confirm what is being deleted BEFORE you click OK.

Deleting a service plan is permanent and that data cannot be recovered.
