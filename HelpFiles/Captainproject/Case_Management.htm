This module is where Users will manage all of the demographic data for clients served, services they receive, and service plans that staff are working with. 

This module is directed at supporting you with gathering quantifiable data and to begin the requirements to keep your agency in compliance.  Well-informed, consistent use of CAPTAIN will enable you to report on an entire scope of efforts and accomplishments of your client population, your agency and its programs, and the community as a whole.  From the information gathered, you will be able to build a synthesized strategy for meeting goals and objectives. 

This environment furnishes a vehicle for selecting fund and department defined client services, inter and intra agency referrals (to and from), results and outcomes. You may also apply a value to a service and track time spent assisting a client.
