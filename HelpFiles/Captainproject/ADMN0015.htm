Site Maintenance Tab 

  Site Maintenance is where you build the Master Site Records for your system.  The Master Site records are used in:

  • Client Intake - to associate the client's application to a specific site
  • Service Activity Posting - to associate the service delivery to a specific site
  • Reporting - when the User wants to look at data from a sorted perspective of the site

Always search the system to ensure that the Site you want to build isn't already there. 

Add a Site in the Site Maintenance Tab

Before you begin to build your Site List confirm that you have selected the Master List to work with.  See the image below.  Master should be in the Year field at the top of the tab.  

  1. The Master List must have every site that you want Users to have access to throughout all modules in the system.  For programs that have a YEAR designation you must also build a year site list for that hierarchy position. 
  2. Click the ADD  button at the very top of the tab in order to open the Add Screen below. 
  3. Enter the information specific to the Site you are building
  4. Assign this Site to the hierarchies you want Users to work with it. 
  5. Click SAVE

   

Edit a Site in the Site Maintenance Tab

  1. Search for an existing Site in the Master List
  2. Select the Site (left click it to make it BLUE and confirm that the arrow is on the very left hand side of the row)
  3. Click the EDIT  button at the very top of the tab to access the Edit Screen
  4. Make your changes/updates to the Site Record
  5. Click SAVE

 

Build a Year Site List in the Site Maintenance Tab

Any program that has a YEAR designed into the program definition will use a YEAR specific site list in the system.  The YEAR site list overrides the Master List during that year.  Any SITE that you want in the YEAR list must first have been added to the Master List.  

This feels like a duplication but it isn't.  Both the MASTER List and the YEAR List have a differents functions in the system.
