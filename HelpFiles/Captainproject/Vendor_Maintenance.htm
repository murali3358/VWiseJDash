Vendor Maintenance Tab

Vendor Maintenance is used to enter all your agency specific Vendor information.  (Such as address, phone number, contact person, and fuel types.)

Always Search the database before adding a new Vendor.  Having more than one Vendor with the same name will confuse your Users.  Once a User has used a Vendor you cannot delete it and a User interacting with reports options will need to duplicate their effort for both Vendor accounts to ensure that all data is being looked at. 

All Vendor codes must be numeric. 

Add a Vendor in the Vendor Maintenance Tab

Fields with a RED asterisk are required.  You must complete them in order to save your work. 

  1. Click the ADD  button at the very top of the Vendor Maintenance Tab. 
  2. Complete all the fields with your Vendor specific information
  3. Click SAVE

Edit a Vendor in the Vendor Maintenance Tab

  1. Search for the Vendor you want to edit. 
  2. Select it (make sure it's Blue and the arrow is at the left hand side of the row)
  3. Click the EDIT  button at the very top of the Vendor Maintenance Tab
  4. Make your updates/changes
  5. Click SAVE
