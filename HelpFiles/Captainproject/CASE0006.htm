Service Activity Posting

     The Service/Activity Details main window shows you all of the Contact records and associated Service/Activity records for the currently selected applicant. The objective is to track the individuals that received services. The services rendered to each individual and household will be reflected in the statistical and demographic reports. Please see the help messages for the Contact Details box and Service/Activity box for more details.

Contact

       The objective of the Contact window is to enter all of the information that is related to the initial contact with the client or contact person that contacted you on behalf of the client.  A contact record must be added before you can add a service/activity record.

Control

Description

Contact Name

Is the name of the person that contacted you in regards to specific services.  If the contact person is a member of the household for the currently selected applicant, click the Search button to the right of this field to select the applicable household member name.  Otherwise, enter the name of the contact person in the field.

# of contacts

Is the total number of times that the contact person contacted you in regards to specific services.  It is an optional field that can be left blank.  However, if a value is entered, it must be a numeric value.

Case Worker

Is the case worker that is interacting with the contact person and/or the client in order to enter the applicable contact information and services for the client.

Contact Date

Represents the date that the contact person originally contacted you in regards to specific services.  You can fill the contact date with today's date by pressing the Page Down key.

Duration Type - E /S Time

The duration type option allows you to track the length of time of the contact using one of two different methods. Select the "Enter Start/End Time" option button if you want to enter the specific start time and end time for the contact's duration. The duration hours and minutes will automatically be calculated. Select the "Enter Duration" option button if you want to manually enter the duration hours and minutes.

Duration Enter Duration

Help for Enter Duration Radio Button .

Start Time

Enter the time that the contact started.  You must enter the hour, minutes and AM/PM separately.  The start time must be less than the end date.

End Time

Enter the time that the contact ended.  You must enter the hour, minutes and AM/PM separately.  The end time must be greater than the start date.

Duration Hrs

Enter the number of hours that the contact lasted.  If the contact was for less than 1 hour, you can leave the duration hours field blank.  However, you must enter either duration hours or minutes before saving the contact information.

Duration Mins

Enter the number of minutes that the contact lasted.  If the contact was exactly 1 hour, you can leave the duration minutes field blank.  However, you must enter either duration hours or minutes before saving the contact information.

How/Where

Select from the drop down box the method of contact or the location that the contact took place.

Language Contact Speaks

Select the language that the contact person spoke during the contact.

Interpreter Needed

Was an interpreter needed when you communicated with the contact person?  Select the applicable response from the drop down box.

Referred From

Is the Agency that the client(s) was referred from to your Program based on the services they inquired.  The agency that appears in this field must exist in the "Agency Referral Database".  Click the Search button to the right of this field to select the applicable Referred From agency.

Search

Click the Search button to display all of the agencies that have been added to the "Agency Referral Database".  A window will appear that will allow you to select the applicable Referred From agency.  The name of the agency will appear in the Referred From field when you select a specific agency from the Agency Referral window.

Bill To

Is the Agency that will be billed for the services that are rendered to the client.  The agency that appears in this field must exist in the "Agency Referral Database".  Click the Search button to the right of this field to select the applicable Bill To agency.

Search

Click the Search button to display all of the agencies that have been added to the "Agency Referral Database".  A window will appear that will allow you to select the applicable Bill To agency.  The name of the agency will appear in the Bill To field when you select a specific agency from the Agency Referral window.

Copy

Click the Copy button to copy the agency name that is currently in the Referred From field to the Bill To field.  The objective is to save you data entry time.  You can also click the Search button to the right of the Bill To field and select the same agency for the Bill To field.

Bill Unit

The Bill Unit is only applicable when the Bill To has been filled.  It represents the unit of measure that will be used when billing the agency that has been selected in the Bill To field.

Interpreter Needed

Was an interpreter needed when you communicated with the contact person?  Select the applicable response from the drop down box.
