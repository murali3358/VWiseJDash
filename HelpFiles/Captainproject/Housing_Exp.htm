View Housing Expenses and Service Inquiry in the Client Intake Screen

The bottom section of the Client Intake Screen is a display/view only area.  If a User has entered any information in these Tabs sections you will be able to select the Tab and review the information.

Add/Edit Housing Expenses and Service Inquiry in the Client Intake Screen

Access the Housing Expenses and Service Inquiry Tab through the EDIT  Button at the top of the Client Intake Screen above the Tabs.  This is where the User enters in housing expenses and also check the box(es) of the services the client was inquiring about.

When you click the EDIT  Button the screen will activate and you can interact with the fields in each Tab Section. 

Depending on the browser you are using, you may find a scroll bar in this section to use. 

Agencies that use the Energy Services module will find a required prompt to complete the Landlord information when selecting RENT as the housing type.  If your agency does not need this feature, please identify to your System Administrator that they need to communicate this to us so we can remove the required function.
