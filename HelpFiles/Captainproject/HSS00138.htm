Bus Master Maintenance

This screen allows you to manage specific information on your bus fleet.  Bus Master Maintenance must be completed before you can assign an enrolled child to a bus/route. 

 

 Several of the fields in the image above are self-explanatory.

  • Description - enter a detailed description of the bus so that it's easy for a User to identify that it is the bus they want.
  • Location 1 & 2 - enter the location of where the bus is stored when not in use.  Location 2 could be an interim location throughout the day or even perhaps alternate days of the week. 
  • Expire Date field - enter the date the lease expires.

Bus Route Maintenance ADD a Route to a Bus

Once the busses have been added to the Bus Master Maintenance file, they will show in the list box below.   The ADD button on the right hand side of each row in the Add Column allows the User to assign a specific route to that bus. 

A Left Mouse click on the ADD button will access the working screen below:

Route Information

  • You will want to set up a Route No. that is assigned to the selected bus.  Each bus can have multiple route numbers if necessary. 
  • Pick up - enter the time that the selected bus/route will pick up the first child.
  • Arrive - the time that the selected bus will arrive at the Center/School/Site.
  • Leave - the time that the selected bus will leave the Center/School/Site.
  • Area Served - enter a brief description to identify the area this bus/route serves.

Driver Information

  • Name - the name of the driver assigned to the bus/route.
  • Enter the driver's Date of Birth.
  • Telephone # - enter the driver's telephone number.
  • Enter the driver's license number and all applicable expiration dates for all certifications this driver holds.

Once you have completed these tasks you can then move to the Bus Client Placement screen to assign children to the Bus Routes.
