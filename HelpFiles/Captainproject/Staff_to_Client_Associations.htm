Staff to Client Associations

  This screen is used to associate specific staff to client applications.

Once you select a specific staff member (resourced from the Staff Master Maintenance records) you will be able to set different filters to interact with the specific client records you need. 

Based on the client list that populates the bottom grid box, you will then right click a specific client to access to following selection choices in the following image:

  • After you assign an association for a specific staff to a client(s) the name of the staff member will appear in the Staff Association box in the bottom left hand corner of the screen. 
  • The number of staff associations a client has will be indicated in the row with the column directly to the left of the App Column.
